A P J Abdul Kalam

BIRTH DETAILS

  • Full name: Abul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam
  • Abul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram
  • He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.
  • Died: 27 July 2015, Shillong

  • Veer Savarkar Award: 1998
